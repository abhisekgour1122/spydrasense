NETRA™ N & T — Device Options Comparison

Side-by-side comparison of the two device options · HealSphere and Muse 2 / Muse S
What the assessment reads: the two indices are built from eleven readings that require two kinds of signal — EEG (brain rhythms) and a heart signal (PPG → HRV). This document compares the two device options on verified facts only. Each row has a short plain-language note. Muse details are from the manufacturer's public documentation; HealSphere details are from the document EEG FOR FUTUREFIRST LEADERSHIP USE ONLY_PDF.pdf and the Heal & Soul technical white paper referenced within it.

The eleven readings — per device

The pattern is the same across all 11: HealSphere provides each reading ready-made; with Muse, we calculate each one ourselves from the raw signal.

Reading names and index mapping are from the specification document (see Sources). HealSphere: produced ready by its pipeline. Muse: derived in our software (HRV readings from PPG; brain-rhythm readings from EEG).
